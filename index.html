things i do

learn more about my many roles as a dev

design

making your project stand out these days in difficult. i use my obsession with pop culture along with a lifetime of user experience to appeal to the user's emotions and instills a familiarity that will drive their love for it's content.

development

whether its small, or large scale, i'll find ways to to increase the flow of advanced functions in and throughout your project and decrease the space, confusion, lack of upgradability that plagues even many large entities.

applications

these days every project, even those non-mobile, has an accompanying app that reflects the beauty and creates a fast, fun portal into their domain. let me help you make yours leave more than just a good impression.

audio/visual

for over 10 years I've used software to create music and performed live across the us, my keen sense for the groove and my extensive studies in audio code allows me to take your audio or video ideas and bring them to lfie

content creation

social media has become a pillar in online marketing, or even talking to your fans. i'll create and help you learn to manage amazing memorable ux experiences. leave a mark on your edge of the internet.

lessons

you may access my library of YouTube videos where i explain some important concepts and demonstrate them for new to intermediate coding hobbyists. want to see something in specific? contact me.
